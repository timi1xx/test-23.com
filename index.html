White Coffee
White coffee is made from lightly roasted or unroasted beans, giving it a much lighter color and a milder, smoother taste compared to traditional coffee. It retains more antioxidants due to the minimal roasting process, making it a healthier option for some. The flavor is less bitter and often described as nutty or earthy. Because of its low acidity, white coffee is ideal for those with sensitive stomachs. Its unique profile has made it increasingly popular among coffee enthusiasts seeking something different.
